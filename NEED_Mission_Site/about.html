About NEED

History, objectives, and the impact that drives our nationwide movement.

History

  1. 2018

    Origins

    Educators and sustainability advocates convene to address the gap in energy literacy and climate action in schools and colleges.

  2. 2020

    Pilot Programs

    Initial pilots in 10 institutions demonstrate measurable savings in energy use and stronger environmental awareness.

  3. 2023

    Expansion

    Partnerships with NGOs and local governments expand NEED into community-level initiatives.

  4. Today

    Nationwide Movement

    NEED now connects students, educators, professionals, and citizens through programs, resources, and open collaboration.

Objectives

  • Build energy and environmental literacy through curriculum-aligned content.
  • Enable hands-on projects that translate learning into measurable action.
  • Foster leadership and networks among students, educators, and practitioners.
  • Support institutions to set targets and track sustainability metrics.
  • Engage communities via awareness campaigns and local drives.
